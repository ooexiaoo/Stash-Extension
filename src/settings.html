Setup Instructions

1

Log in to Stashed.in

Click the "Log In to Stashed.in" button above to open the login page in your browser.

2

Go to Edit Profile

After logging in, navigate to your profile settings by clicking your avatar → Edit Profile.

3

Generate Extension Token

Scroll to the "Browser Extension Access" section and click "Generate API Token" or "Copy Token".

4

Paste Token Here

Return to this page and paste your token in the "Manual Token Entry" section above, then click "Save Token".

💡 Pro Tip: Your extension token is permanent and works across all devices. Unlike browser sessions, it won't expire when you clear cache or switch browsers!

Your token is stored locally on this device only and never shared.
